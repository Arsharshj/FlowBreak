FlowBreak

Guide

Hi! FlowBreak is a productivity tool app that will allow you to get more work done instantly. It’s really simple to start. This timer was based on the Pomodoro Technique. The traditional and basic way of this Technique is the following:

  • Pick A Task
  • Set a 25 minute timer
  • Work on your task until the time is up
  • Take a 5 minute break
  • Every 4 cycles, take a longer 25 minute break

Except with this timer, everything is customizable. You can change the amount of time you work for, the amount of time for the small breaks, and the amount of time for the long break. For example, if you want to just use the Break Timer, I would recommend you put in “0.05” in for the Work Timer, in order to get to the Break Timer as fast as possible.

However, there is a twist that I added that is very important in this day and age. When you are on your breaks, you can open a Website, and then when the time ends, it closes automatically, breaking the trend of taking a break, and forgetting about your task all together.

Once you are done, you can click the Stop button at the bottom of the timer. Remember it is important to do this, as you won’t get your gold for the time you worked if you don’t click the button.

You can use the gold that you earned through your hard work at the Shop! Just click on the shop, and if you have enough gold (every background is 300 gold which is approximately 5 minutes of work), you can get it and change to it whenever you want.

I hope this tool changes your productivity for the better!
